Venado Declaration (Timber Products)

In collaboration with the Timber Products company and group members, we proposed potential timber mills locations that can process low value material to address biomass build up in the Shasta and Siskiyou counties. Our report tackles the issue of “how and where do we implement the urgent actions called for in the Venado Declaration to encourage and incentivize expansion of infrastructure to enable a substantial and rapid increase in timberland management to protect communities and improve forest health.”[More...]
